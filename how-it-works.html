Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take? ▼
We can get you an offer in 24 hours. Closing typically happens in 5-30 days, depending on your preference. If you need more time to relocate or settle other matters, we're happy to work with you. You're always in control of the timeline.
What types of properties do you buy? ▼
We buy single-family homes, multi-family properties, investment homes, distressed properties, inherited homes, and probate sales. Whether your home needs work or is in perfect condition, we're interested. If it's in the Bay Area, we likely can help.
What areas do you service? ▼
We operate throughout the South Bay and Peninsula in the San Francisco Bay Area, including San Jose, San Mateo, Palo Alto, Sunnyvale, Cupertino, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm we serve your area.
Do I have to make repairs before selling? ▼
No repairs needed. We buy homes as-is. Whether your home has cosmetic wear, structural issues, or needs major updates, we'll handle it. You save the time, money, and stress of renovations.
How do you determine your offer price? ▼
We analyze comparable sales, the property's condition, market trends, and closing costs. Our offer reflects fair market value minus any repairs we'll need to make. We're transparent about our offer and happy to explain our reasoning.
Are there any hidden fees or surprises? ▼
No. Our offer is all-in. No hidden fees, no surprise costs at closing. What we quote is what you get. We handle all closing costs. Total transparency is part of our promise.
What if I need to stay in the home after selling? ▼
We can work with you on a rent-back arrangement. You can stay in your home after closing and pay rent until you're ready to leave. Let us know your needs, and we'll find a solution.
How do I get started? ▼
It's easy! Call us, email us, or fill out our online form. Tell us about your property, and we'll schedule a property evaluation at your convenience. From there, we'll get you an offer within 24 hours. No obligation.
